WHO ARE WE

Okir Publishing is a printing and distribution service provider of paperback, hardback, and e-books in numerous formats. Currently based in Cebu, Philippines, Okir Publishing started out as a marketing services provider in 2006 and eventually decided to venture out into the publishing business later on.

As a publishing services provider, we accept manuscripts of different genres while ensuring that the full ownership of the book remains with the author.


WHAT WE DO

Okir Publishing provides excellence in customer service, while offering a wide array of different packages and services for you to jumpstart your career in publishing your book to ensure that your book gets the attention that it deserves.

VISION & MISSION STATEMENT

Our Mission

Okir Publishing's mission is to provide exceptional, innovative and up-to-date services for our clients and ensure that each of them will be given an individualized solution to all their needs to stand-out in the publishing world. The company aims to help publish books that can make a difference in the world and promote a culture that will nurture and attract talented individuals into the team, all while being a responsible corporate entity that balances the interests and needs of our clients, the stakeholders, and our community.

Our Vision

Okir Publishing aspires to help authors achieve their dream of making it in the publishing world a reality. It envisions to be a leading developer, publisher, and provider of globally competitive books and to be recognized as the primary provider of excellent service for authors who aspire to create a great impact in the literary world.
