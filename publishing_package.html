Okir Marketing Services offers selections of different publishing packages specifically made for your hassle-free journey to the world of literature. We ensure that after your book has been written and edited, we take the job of completing the interior and cover designs of the book and making sure that it gets noticed by online stores and major book distributors. This also includes printing-on-demand as all the orders come in, while ensuring that all the rights to your book at every stage still belongs to you.

BASIC

This package provides all the essential elements and options to make sure that your manuscript becomes a quality paperback book worth investing your time to read through. Your work will be included in the book industry's leading book distributors online and can be ordered through retail and online stores like BarnesandNoble.com and Amazon.com.

The Basic service includes:


  • Design and image features:

    • A distribution of author-supplied cover images and author photo
    • A choice of eight (8) book cover templates
    • A choice of five (5) interior templates

  • Post-publication features:

    • A paperback Author copy and five (5) paperback copies
    • Google Books Preview
    • Registration with Books In Print® database
    • Amazon Look Inside
    • Barnes and Noble Read Instantly
    • Worldwide Online Book Distribution
    • International Standard Book Number (ISBN) assignment
    • Accounting of online book sales and royalty
    • Quarterly royalty payments

  • Production features:

    • Availability of your book in both paperback and e-book formats
    • Ability to monitor the book's production process through the website
    • Electronic galley
    • A round of book revisions & corrections services (up to 25)

  • Marketing Features:

    • 50 postcards
    • 50 business cards
    • 50 bookmarks
    • 5 posters
Disclaimer: While the package includes the cost of the free books, you will be responsible to pay for the shipping and handling costs.
