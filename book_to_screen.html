    Hollywood Ticket

    $999.00

    Fancy your book to be one of the next great movie or television series? Avail the Hollywood Ticket service to give your book a chance for a break in the entertainment industry.

    So how does it work? A professional reader will read and evaluate your book if it is cut-out to become an adaptation. Coverage will be used to identify and analyze your book’s adaptation potential. Your Coverage will consist of the following elements:

    1. A detailed synopsis highlighting the main characters and events in your story.
    2. An analysis addressing key story points and your book’s feasibility if adapted to another medium. Production costs, genre, storylines, and characters are important factors considered during the book evaluation.

    One the Coverage of your book is done, it will be submitted to a first-look partner who will determine if your book can become an option for a movie, television, or digital content adaptation.

    Your book’s Coverage will also be placed in an exclusive database used by executives in the entertainment industry to search for fresh and exciting material.

    *Note that this service will be fulfilled by an independent professional who also does assessments for major agencies in the industry and Okir Marketing Services will have no input about the analysis done on your book.

    Hollywood Director's Cut

    $3,899.00

    We’re sure your book has all the chief elements to become a Hollywood hit – a great plot, strong and appealing characters, and exciting content. But there is still an important factor that must not be forgotten for you to be really heard by executives in the entertainment industry - a Treatment.

    A Treatment is a meticulously created long-form pitch for a movie, television, or digital series that summarizes how a producer would make your book into a film or television adaptation. A professional screenwriter experienced in book-to-movie adaptations will write the treatment with your direction on how they will start.

    When you exactly know the approach of your book’s adaptation, you reduce the work of agents and producers thus enticing them to consider reading through your book. You give them blow-by-blow of your book’s translation for the screen and clearly show that your storyline has the potential to be a hit.

    Once done, your Treatment will be submitted to a first-look partner who will be responsible for determining if your work can become an option for a movie, television, or digital content adaptation. Your book’s Treatment will also be placed in an exclusive database used by executives in the entertainment industry to search for fresh and exciting material.

    Fret not about your story’s ownership. The screenwriter waives all rights to the treatment, so you have full ownership of it.

    Make your dreams into a reality and take the first steps to make your book into a hit in the big-screen!

    While it is known to be a fully developed and complete script, a screenplay is a document that entertainment executives use to determine if your book’s adaptation is something they want to fund.

    Once your Hollywood Producer’s Pick is done, your work will be submitted to a first-look partner who will be responsible for determining if your work can become an option for a movie, television, or digital content adaptation.
